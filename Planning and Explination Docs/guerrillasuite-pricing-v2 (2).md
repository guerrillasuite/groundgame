## User Role Tiers

Every GuerrillaSuite product uses the same four-level user role system. Roles are consistent across products — a Director in GroundGame is a Director in LedgerLine.

| Role | Description | Availability |
|------|-------------|--------------|
| **Operative** | Field-level user. Works lists, logs dispositions, uses Storefront View, sees SitRep. No management access. | All tiers, unlimited |
| **Support** | Mid-level operations user. Cuts lists, manages teams, views reports, assigns tasks. Cannot create users or access HQ/system settings. | All tiers, per seat |
| **Director** | Full admin. System owner for their org. Creates users, manages settings, full HQ access, everything their tier unlocks. | All tiers, per seat (included count varies by tier) |
| **General** | Enterprise and National only. Cross-county and cross-campaign oversight. Bird's eye view across all entities under a contract. Not a system config role — visibility and coordination only. | Enterprise + National only |

### 🌐 National Account
**Best for:** National parties and organizations coordinating multiple state affiliates

The National account is a parent layer that connects state Enterprise accounts, provides General-level oversight across all affiliates, and unlocks state-level discounts based on how many states are signed. It is separate from the National org's own tenant account.

**National umbrella pricing by states signed:**

| States Signed | National Account Fee | State Discount |
|--------------|---------------------|----------------|
| 1–4 states | No national account — standard Enterprise per state | None |
| 5–24 states | $2,500/year | 10% off each state Enterprise account |
| 25–49 states | $1,500/year | 20% off each state Enterprise account |
| 50 states | Free | 25% off each state Enterprise account |

**National org tenant account:**
The national organization also gets its own full tenant — their own data, their own campaigns (presidential, fundraising, membership, etc.) — priced as a standard Enterprise account based on their own record volume. At true national scale (300M+ records) pricing is a custom conversation above the $10K hard cap.

- 5 Directors included
- Additional Directors: $10/seat/month
- National orgs with fewer than 5 state affiliates signed are capped at 50M records on their own tenant

## Arsenal Pricing

Arsenal is not sold as a standalone product. It is available as an add-on to GroundGame or LedgerLine, and is included free when both are held at War Chest level or higher.

### Add-on Pricing
- **With GroundGame only:** +$30/month (any GG tier)
- **With LedgerLine only:** +$30/month (Company tier or higher)
- **With both GroundGame + LedgerLine at War Chest+:** Included free

### What Arsenal Includes
- Inventory management & stock level tracking
- Barcode scanning
- Product catalog management
- Purchase order tracking
- Order fulfillment workflow
- eCommerce storefront (online selling)
- Unified order history with GroundGame Storefront View
- Automatic income feed into LedgerLine

## Multi-Tenant Discounts

Users or organizations managing multiple unrelated tenant accounts receive a loyalty discount on additional accounts. This applies to separate, unrelated orgs — not child tenants under an Enterprise or National account.

| Additional Tenants | Discount |
|-------------------|----------|
| 2nd tenant | 10% off that tenant's base plan |
| 3rd+ tenants | 15% off each additional tenant |

Personal and Household LedgerLine tiers do not qualify for multi-tenant discounts.

---

## Bundle Pricing

Bundle discounts apply when a tenant holds multiple GuerrillaSuite products. Discounts are applied to the lower-priced product.

| Combination | Discount |
|-------------|----------|
| GroundGame + LedgerLine (any matching tier) | 15% off LedgerLine |
| GroundGame + LedgerLine at War Chest+ | Arsenal included free |
| GroundGame + Arsenal add-on | Standard add-on rate |
| LedgerLine + Arsenal add-on | Standard add-on rate |
| GroundGame + LedgerLine + Arsenal at War Chest+ | All three, Arsenal free |

## Resolved Decisions

1. **Arsenal at Enterprise** — Free. Enterprise clients get Arsenal included at no additional cost.

2. **LedgerLine annual discounts** — 1.5–2 months free model, consistent with GroundGame annual pricing.

3. **SitRep standalone pricing** — Free to all users when it spins off as a standalone product, including Operatives on Scout Kit and anyone outside GuerrillaSuite entirely. Modeled after Google Calendar.

4. **Intel Brief standalone pricing** — Free to all users when it spins off as a standalone product, same model as SitRep.

5. **Presidential campaign accounts** — No special Presidential account tier needed. Pre-nomination candidates each get a standard War Chest account ($100/month) as isolated tenants. 250K registered Libertarians fits comfortably inside the 1M contact ceiling. Post-nomination, the nominee upgrades to an Enterprise account if voter file scale is needed, or connects to the National party umbrella if one exists. Pre-nomination accounts are intentionally identical — no candidate gets a structural advantage through the platform.
